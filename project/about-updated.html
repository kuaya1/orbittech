About The Orbit Tech

Your Trusted Partner for Professional Satellite Internet Installation

Professional Installation Experts

The Orbit Tech is a leading provider of professional Starlink and Amazon Kuiper installation services in the DMV area. Based in McLean, Virginia, we specialize in delivering reliable, high-speed satellite internet solutions for homes and businesses.

Our team of certified technicians brings years of experience in satellite communications and installation, ensuring your system is set up correctly for optimal performance from day one.

We understand that internet connectivity is essential for modern life and business operations. That's why we're committed to providing fast, professional installation services that get you connected quickly and reliably.

Comprehensive Service Coverage

From our base in McLean, VA, we serve customers throughout the Washington DC metropolitan area, including Northern Virginia, Maryland, and the District of Columbia. Our service area extends to ensure professional installation support wherever you need it.

Whether you're upgrading from traditional internet, need reliable connectivity for remote work, or require business-grade satellite internet solutions, we have the expertise and equipment to meet your needs.
