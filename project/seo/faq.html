The Installation Process

What does a typical Starlink installation involve?

Our professional installation process is comprehensive yet efficient. Here's what we handle for you:

  • Site Assessment: We evaluate your property to find the optimal dish location with clear sky visibility
  • Professional Mounting: Secure installation using appropriate mounting hardware for your roof, pole, or ground setup
  • Cable Routing: Clean, weatherproof cable runs from the dish to your desired indoor location
  • System Setup: Complete configuration of your Starlink system, including Wi-Fi network setup
  • Testing & Optimization: Speed tests and signal verification to ensure peak performance
  • Customer Training: We'll show you how to use the Starlink app and manage your system

Our certified technicians handle everything, so you don't have to worry about any technical details or safety concerns.

How long does a Starlink installation take?

Most installations are completed in 2-4 hours, depending on your specific setup:

  • Standard residential (2-3 hours): Ground mount or simple roof installation
  • Complex residential (3-4 hours): Multi-story homes, challenging roof access, or custom pole mounts
  • Commercial installations (4-6 hours): Larger buildings, multiple access points, or specialized requirements

We always provide an accurate time estimate during your free consultation, and our team works efficiently to minimize disruption to your day.

Do you handle all the drilling and wiring?

Absolutely! Our experienced technicians handle all aspects of the physical installation, including:

  • Professional drilling: Precise holes for mounting hardware and cable entry points
  • Weatherproofing: All penetrations are properly sealed to prevent water damage
  • Cable management: Clean routing through walls, attics, or crawlspaces as needed
  • Safety compliance: All work meets local building codes and safety standards

We come fully equipped with professional tools and take care to protect your property during installation. No need for you to purchase or provide any tools or hardware.

Do you perform a site survey before installation?

Yes, we conduct a thorough site assessment as part of every installation. This includes:

  • Sky visibility analysis using professional tools
  • Structural evaluation for safe mounting options
  • Cable routing planning for the cleanest installation
  • Discussion of your specific internet needs and usage

This ensures we provide the best possible installation for your unique property and requirements. Our professional assessment eliminates guesswork and guarantees optimal performance.

Equipment and Setup

What comes in the Starlink kit?

The standard Starlink kit includes everything needed for basic operation:

  • Starlink dish: The satellite receiver with built-in motors for positioning
  • Wi-Fi router: Provides wireless internet throughout your home
  • Power supply: Weather-resistant power adapter
  • 75-foot cable: Connects the dish to your router
  • Kickstand: For temporary ground placement
  • Setup materials: Quick start guide and app instructions

While the kit includes a basic kickstand, our professional installation provides secure, permanent mounting solutions that ensure optimal performance and longevity.

Will I need to buy anything extra?

For most installations, no additional equipment purchase is required. We provide:

  • Professional mounting hardware (included in installation fee)
  • Weather-resistant cable connections
  • Grounding equipment where required
  • Cable routing materials

Optional upgrades available:

  • Extended cable lengths for longer runs
  • Ethernet adapters for wired connections
  • Surge protection devices
  • Mesh network extenders for large homes

We'll discuss any recommended upgrades during your consultation based on your specific needs and property layout.

Where is the best place to install the Starlink dish?

The optimal location depends on your property, but generally we look for:

  • Clear sky view: Unobstructed view of the northern sky (most important)
  • Minimal obstructions: Away from trees, buildings, or other structures
  • Stable mounting surface: Roof peak, side of house, or dedicated pole
  • Reasonable cable run: Close enough to route cables to your desired internet location

Our technicians use professional tools to analyze your property and identify the perfect spot. We consider both performance and aesthetics to find a location that works well and looks good.

Can I keep my existing internet during the transition?

Absolutely! We recommend keeping your current internet active until you're satisfied with Starlink performance. Here's why:

  • Test Starlink speeds and reliability for your specific usage
  • Ensure all your devices and applications work properly
  • Have backup internet during any adjustment period
  • Make the transition seamless for work or family needs

Most customers run both services for 1-2 weeks to ensure complete satisfaction before canceling their previous provider. Our installation won't interfere with your existing internet service.

Performance and Reliability

How does Starlink perform in bad weather?

Starlink performs remarkably well in most weather conditions common to the DMV area:

  • Light rain/snow: No noticeable impact on performance
  • Heavy rain: May experience brief speed reductions (usually 10-20%)
  • Snow accumulation: Dish has built-in heating to melt snow automatically
  • High winds: No impact on service (our secure mounting prevents movement)

Any weather-related slowdowns are typically brief and service automatically returns to full speed when conditions improve. Our professional installation includes weatherproofing that protects all components from the elements.

What kind of internet speeds can I expect?

Starlink users in the DMV region typically experience excellent speeds:

  • Download speeds: 100-250 Mbps (sufficient for 4K streaming, video calls, gaming)
  • Upload speeds: 10-25 Mbps (great for video conferencing and cloud backups)
  • Latency: 25-50ms (suitable for real-time gaming and video calls)
  • Data: Unlimited with no throttling or data caps

Our professional installation ensures you achieve the best possible speeds by optimizing dish placement and eliminating interference. We test your speeds before leaving to verify optimal performance.

How reliable is Starlink service?

Starlink has proven to be highly reliable in the DMV area, with most users experiencing:

  • 99%+ uptime: Service interruptions are rare and brief
  • Consistent performance: Speeds remain stable throughout the day
  • Self-healing network: Automatically switches between satellites for continuous coverage
  • No infrastructure dependencies: Not affected by local cable cuts or equipment failures

Our professional installation eliminates common reliability issues by ensuring proper grounding, secure mounting, and optimal positioning. This gives you the most dependable Starlink experience possible.

Are there any data limits or throttling?

Starlink residential service offers truly unlimited data with no throttling:

  • No monthly data caps or limits
  • No speed throttling after high usage
  • No additional charges for heavy data use
  • Perfect for streaming, gaming, working from home, and large downloads

This makes Starlink ideal for households with high internet usage, multiple users, or those who work from home. Our installation ensures you can take full advantage of this unlimited service with optimal performance.

Is Starlink suitable for business use?

Yes! Starlink is excellent for business applications in the DMV area:

  • Remote work: Reliable video conferencing and VPN connections
  • Small businesses: Support for point-of-sale systems and cloud applications
  • Backup internet: Redundant connection for critical business operations
  • Rural locations: High-speed internet where traditional options are limited

Our commercial installation service includes business-grade mounting, enhanced weatherproofing, and coordination with your IT requirements. We understand business needs and ensure minimal disruption during installation.
