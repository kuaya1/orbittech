Starlink Equipment Overview

Understanding your Starlink kit components is essential for a successful installation in the DMV area. Each kit includes the satellite dish (nicknamed "Dishy"), power supply, router, and necessary cables.

What's Included in Your Starlink Kit:

  • Starlink Dish: The main satellite receiver with built-in motors for positioning
  • Base/Mount: Standard mounting base for ground or deck installation
  • Power Supply: Weather-resistant power adapter
  • Router: Wi-Fi 6 capable router for home networking
  • Cables: 75-foot cable with proprietary connectors
