Arlington County Starlink Installation FAQs

Can Pentagon contractors safely use Starlink for backup internet?

Yes! We understand OPSEC requirements and provide secure installation practices. Starlink is an excellent backup for non-classified internet needs, with automatic failover to maintain operational security during primary outages.

Can I install Starlink in my Crystal City high-rise apartment?

Absolutely! We specialize in high-rise installations and handle all building management coordination, roof access permits, and insurance requirements. We have established relationships with most major Arlington buildings.

How does Starlink help Arlington's remote federal workers?

Critical for telework reliability! When Comcast/Verizon experience outages, federal employees can't connect to secure networks. Starlink provides automatic backup ensuring continuous VPN access and video conference capability.

What's the installation process for Arlington high-rises?

We handle everything: building management approval, roof access coordination, insurance documentation, and professional installation. Most Arlington buildings approve within 5-7 days. Installation typically completed in 2-4 hours.
